# Fluo: Learn Vocabulary — Support

Thank you for using **Fluo**. If you need help, run into a problem, or want to share feedback, use the contact below.

## Contact

**Email:** [batu.develops@gmail.com](mailto:batu.develops@gmail.com?subject=Fluo%20Support)

Please include:

- A short description of the issue  
- Your device model and iOS version (if relevant)  
- Screenshots (if helpful)

We try to respond as soon as we can.

## Privacy & terms

Privacy Policy and Terms of Use are available **inside the app** (Settings or legal sections, depending on your version).

## Subscriptions

**Fluo Premium** is an optional auto-renewable subscription. You can manage or cancel it in your **Apple ID** subscription settings. You can also use **Restore purchase** in the app’s **Settings** if you reinstall or change devices.
